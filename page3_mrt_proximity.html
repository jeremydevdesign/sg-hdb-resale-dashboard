The "Over 2km" band commands surprisingly high prices in some towns (Punggol, Tampines, Pasir Ris) — not a proximity premium but a composition effect: flats far from MRT in these towns tend to be newer, larger builds in less dense areas. The ≤200m band reflects genuine station-proximity value, strongest for 4-room and executive flats.
